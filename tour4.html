Tour 4

HILL AND COAST LOOP — 8 DAYS / 7 NIGHTS

A scenic route from Negombo through Kandy, Sigiriya, Anuradhapura and Ella before finishing on the southern coast.

TOUR ITINERARY

DAY 01 | AIRPORT to NEGOMBO

Arrive in Sri Lanka and transfer to Negombo for your first overnight stay.

DAY 02 | NEGOMBO to KANDY

Travel inland to Kandy and enjoy the hill-country atmosphere and cultural highlights.

DAY 03 | KANDY to SIGIRIYA

Head to Sigiriya and settle into the Cultural Triangle for an evening stay.

DAY 04 | SIGIRIYA to ANURADHAPURA

Continue to Anuradhapura to explore the ancient city and sacred heritage sites.

DAY 05 | ANURADHAPURA to ELLA

Travel south-east to Ella for cool mountain air and a relaxed evening in the hills.

DAY 06 | ELLA to UNAWATUNA

Descend to the southern coast and unwind in Unawatuna with beach time and sea views.

DAY 07 | UNAWATUNA to NEGOMBO

Return north to Negombo for a final overnight near the airport.

DAY 08 | NEGOMBO to AIRPORT

Transfer to the airport for departure.
