Tour 2

COAST TO HILL CIRCUIT — 6 DAYS / 5 NIGHTS

A compact Sri Lanka journey from ancient Sigiriya and Kandy to Ella, Mirissa and Bentota, ending back at the airport.

TOUR ITINERARY

DAY 01 | AIRPORT to SIGIRIYA

Arrive in Sri Lanka and travel directly to Sigiriya for your first overnight stay near the Cultural Triangle.

DAY 02 | SIGIRIYA to KANDY

Visit Sigiriya and continue to Kandy for an evening among the hill-country capital’s cultural highlights.

DAY 03 | KANDY to ELLA

Journey through tea country to Ella and enjoy the scenic mountain setting on arrival.

DAY 04 | ELLA to MIRISSA

Travel down to the southern coast and settle into Mirissa for beach time and relaxation.

DAY 05 | MIRISSA to BENTOTA

Continue along the coast to Bentota for river, beach and leisure experiences.

DAY 06 | BENTOTA to AIRPORT

Transfer to the airport for departure after your 6-day, 5-night Sri Lanka journey.
