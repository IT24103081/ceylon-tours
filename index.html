Who We Are

Golden Island Tours

We are a Sri Lanka-based travel company with offices in Italy and Sri Lanka, specialising in handcrafted private tours for international travellers. From 14-day signature routes to honeymoon escapes and coastal journeys, every trip is designed with care, local knowledge, and 24/7 on-the-ground support.

Founded on a passion for Sri Lanka's extraordinary diversity — its wildlife, heritage, landscapes, and people — we connect travellers with authentic experiences they won't find in a brochure.
