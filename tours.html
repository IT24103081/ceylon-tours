SRI LANKA SPLENDORS

Discover the splendors of Sri Lanka, its beautiful landscapes, rich history, and incredible wildlife!

09 Nights /10 Days
INQUIRE NOW
DAY 01 | COLOMBO

Upon arrival in Sri Lanka, you will be met and assisted by your guide. Transfer to your hotel in Colombo for a restful evening.

DAY 02 | TEMPLE OF THE TOOTH | KANDY

Visit the sacred Temple of the Tooth Relic in the morning and explore the historic city of Kandy.

DAY 03 | KANDY CITY TOUR ~ BOTANICAL GARDENS | KANDY

Explore the stunning Peradeniya Royal Botanical Gardens and enjoy a cultural dance show in the evening.

DAY 04 | TEA FACTORY VISIT ~ NUWARA ELIYA CITY TOUR | NUWARA ELIYA

Head to the highlands, visit a lush tea estate, learn about the Ceylon tea process, and explore the quaint town of Nuwara Eliya.

DAY 05 | HORTON PLAINS | NUWARA ELIYA

Early morning trek in Horton Plains National Park to witness World's End.

DAY 06 | YALA

Journey down to the dry zone and check into your wildlife lodge bordering Yala National Park.

DAY 07 | WILDLIFE SAFARI | YALA

Morning and evening 4x4 safaris in search of Sri Lankan leopards, elephants, and bears.

DAY 08 | GALLE CITY TOUR | BENTOTA

Travel along the south coast, explore the Dutch Fort of Galle (UNESCO), and relax in Bentota.

DAY 09 | BENTOTA

A full day at leisure on the golden beaches of Bentota.

DAY 10 | DEPARTURE

Transfer to Bandaranaike International Airport for your departure flight.

When entering a Buddhist or Hindu temple, travellers are required to dress respectfully in modest light coloured clothing, with shoulders & knees covered.

No hats or footwear are allowed inside temples, so flip flops (which you would have to leave at the entrance) and an extra pair of socks are a good idea so you do not have to walk barefoot on the hot ground.
