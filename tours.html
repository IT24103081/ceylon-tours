SRI LANKA SPLENDORS

Discover the splendors of Sri Lanka, its beautiful landscapes, rich history, and incredible wildlife!

DURATION 05 Nights / 06 Days
GROUP SIZE Private Tour
TOUR TYPE Culture & Wildlife

TOUR ITINERARY

DAY 01 | GALLE

2 nights in Galle with a 5-star villa stay, breakfast at the villa, Galle Fort, Unawatuna Beach or Jungle Beach, Japanese Peace Pagoda, Habaraduwa Turtle Hatchery, Koggala Lake safari, and traditional stilt fishing.

DAY 02 | ANURADHAPURA

Explore Ruwanwelisaya, Jaya Sri Maha Bodhi, Thuparamaya, Jethawanaramaya, Abhayagiriya, and Tissa Lake.

DAY 03 | POLONNARUWA

Discover Vatadage, Gal Viharaya, Parakrama Samudra, Minneriya National Park, and Kaudulla Lake.

DAY 04 | KANDY

Visit the Temple of the Tooth, Royal Botanic Gardens Peradeniya, Ramboda Falls, and Victoria Dam.

DAY 05 | NUWARA ELIYA

Visit Horton Plains National Park, Gregory Park, and Sandathenna.

DAY 06 | ELLA

Explore Nine Arch Bridge, Little Adam's Peak, Ella Rock, Ravana Cave, Ella Waterfall, Kubalwela Temple, Flying Ravana Adventure Park, and Dunhinda Falls.
