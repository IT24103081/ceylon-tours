SRI LANKA SPLENDORS

Discover the splendors of Sri Lanka, its beautiful landscapes, rich history, and incredible wildlife!

DURATION 14-Day Private Tour
GROUP SIZE Private Tour
TOUR TYPE Culture & Wildlife

TOUR ITINERARY

DAY 01 | GALLE

2 nights in Galle with a 5-star villa stay, breakfast at the villa, Galle Fort, Unawatuna Beach or Jungle Beach, Japanese Peace Pagoda, Habaraduwa Turtle Hatchery, Koggala Lake safari, and traditional stilt fishing.

DAY 02 | UDAWALAWE

Morning safari in Udawalawe, one night near the park, breakfast and lunch from the villa, and dinner out.

DAY 03 | YALA

Leopard-focused safari in Yala, eco stay near the park, and all meals from the villa.

DAY 04 | ARUGAM BAY

Surfing and lagoon experience in Arugam Bay with a cabana stay and meals from a 5-star hotel.

DAY 05 | POLONNARUWA

Travel to the historic city of Polonnaruwa with sacred Polonnaruwa Vatadage, impressive rock-carved Buddha statues at Gal Vihara, expansive Parakrama Samudra ancient reservoir, and evening wildlife safari at Minneriya or Kaudulla National Park for elephant gatherings. Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner at selected 5-star rated hotels.

DAY 06 | HABARANA

Travel to Habarana for a peaceful village experience with lakes, rural life, and all meals at selected 5-star rated hotels.

DAY 07 | SIGIRIYA & DAMBULLA

Sigiriya Rock Fortress and Dambulla Cave Temple with ancient frescoes, gardens, and all meals at selected 5-star rated hotels.

DAY 08 | ANURADHAPURA

Visit Ruwanwelisaya, Jaya Sri Maha Bodhi, Thuparamaya, Jetavanaramaya, and Tissa Wewa with all meals at selected 5-star rated hotels.

DAY 09 | KANDY

Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic and Royal Botanic Gardens, Peradeniya, with breakfast and lunch at selected 5-star rated hotels.

DAY 10 | NUWARA ELIYA

Little England scenery with Horton Plains, Gregory Lake, and tea plantations, with all meals at selected 5-star rated hotels.

DAY 11 | ELLA

Nine Arch Bridge and Little Adam’s Peak in Ella with a selected 5-star rated or boutique stay.

DAY 12 | ELLA ADVENTURE

Ella Rock, Ravana Cave, Ravana Falls, and Flying Ravana Adventure Park with all meals at selected 5-star rated hotels.

DAY 13 | HAPUTALE & BALANGODA

Scenic hill-country viewpoints through Haputale and Balangoda with meals at selected 5-star rated hotels.

DAY 14 | NEGOMBO

Final transfer to Negombo for departure and a relaxed end to the tour by the coast.
