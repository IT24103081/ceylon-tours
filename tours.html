SRI LANKA SPLENDORS

Discover the splendors of Sri Lanka, its beautiful landscapes, rich history, and incredible wildlife!

DURATION 14-Day Private Tour
GROUP SIZE Private Tour
TOUR TYPE Culture & Wildlife

TOUR ITINERARY

DAY 01 | GALLE

2 nights in Galle with a 5-star villa stay, breakfast at the villa, Galle Fort, Unawatuna Beach or Jungle Beach, Japanese Peace Pagoda, Habaraduwa Turtle Hatchery, Koggala Lake safari, and traditional stilt fishing.

DAY 02 | UDAWALAWE

Morning safari in Udawalawe, one night near the park, breakfast and lunch from the villa, and dinner out.

DAY 03 | YALA

Leopard-focused safari in Yala, eco stay near the park, and all meals from the villa.

DAY 04 | ARUGAM BAY

Surfing and lagoon experience in Arugam Bay with a cabana stay and meals from a 5-star hotel.

DAY 05 | POLONNARUWA

Vatadage, Gal Viharaya, Parakrama Samudra, Minneriya National Park, Kaudulla Lake, and a 5-star hotel stay.

DAY 06 | HABARANA

Village experience in Habarana with a 5-star hotel base and all meals from the hotel.

DAY 07 | SIGIRIYA & DAMBULLA

Sigiriya Rock Fortress, Dambulla Cave Temple, and a second Habarana-area night with meals from the hotel.

DAY 08 | ANURADHAPURA

Ruwanwelisaya, Jaya Sri Maha Bodhi, Thuparamaya, Jethawanaramaya, Abhayagiriya, and Tissa Lake.

DAY 09 | KANDY

Temple of the Tooth, Royal Botanic Gardens Peradeniya, and a comfortable 5-star city stay.

DAY 10 | NUWARA ELIYA

Horton Plains National Park, Gregory Park, Sandathenna, and a cool-climate hotel stay.

DAY 11 | ELLA

Nine Arch Bridge, Little Adam's Peak, Ella Rock, and Ravana Cave with a 5-star or boutique stay.

DAY 12 | ELLA ADVENTURE

Ella Waterfall, Kubalwela Temple, Flying Ravana Adventure Park, and a second night in Ella.

DAY 13 | HAPUTALE & BALANGODA

Scenic hill-country viewpoints through Haputale and an overnight stay in Balangoda.

DAY 14 | NEGOMBO

Final transfer to Negombo for departure and a relaxed end to the tour.
