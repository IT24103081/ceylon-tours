EAST COAST PACKAGE

Year-round 10-day 9-night east coast journey with beaches, history, wildlife, and relaxing coastal stays.

10-DAY EAST COAST ITINERARY

DAY 01 | NEGOMBO (1 NIGHT)

Begin your journey in Negombo with a sunset walk at the beach, a relaxing lagoon boat ride, and visits to Muthurajawela Wetlands, the Dutch canal, and the lively fish market.

DAY 02 & DAY 03 | SIGIRIYA (2 NIGHTS)

Travel to Sigiriya for a cultural and scenic stay. Visit Sigiriya Rock Fortress, explore Dambulla Cave Temple, and enjoy a village experience or safari at nearby Minneriya National Park for possible elephant sightings.

DAY 04 & DAY 05 | TRINCOMALEE (2 NIGHTS)

Continue to Trincomalee for beautiful beaches and clear waters. Relax at Nilaveli or Uppuveli Beach, and enjoy Pigeon Island National Park for snorkeling or seasonal whale and dolphin watching.

DAY 06 & DAY 07 | PASIKUDAH (2 NIGHTS)

Travel to Pasikudah, known for shallow calm waters and long sandy beaches. Enjoy a quiet luxury beach stay with time to swim, relax, and enjoy the peaceful coastline.

DAY 08 & DAY 09 | ARUGAM BAY (2 NIGHTS)

Continue to Arugam Bay, one of Sri Lanka’s best surfing destinations. Enjoy surfing, bird watching, lagoon boat rides, and the laid-back beach atmosphere that combines adventure and relaxation.

DAY 10 | RETURN TO NEGOMBO (DEPARTURE)

Return to Negombo for your departure and enjoy your final moments in Sri Lanka after an unforgettable east coast journey.
