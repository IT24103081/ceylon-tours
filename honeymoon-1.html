ROMANTIC SRI LANKA GETAWAY

DAY 01 & DAY 02 | NEGOMBO (2 NIGHTS)

Begin your honeymoon in Negombo, a charming coastal gateway. Explore Negombo Beach with romantic sunset walks, take a peaceful boat ride through Negombo Lagoon surrounded by birdlife, discover the tranquil beauty of Muthurajawela Wetlands, and visit the historic Dutch Canal and vibrant fish market. Perfect for a gentle romantic start to your journey together.

DAY 03 & DAY 04 | BENTOTA (2 NIGHTS)

Travel to Bentota for golden beaches and coastal luxury. Enjoy Bentota Beach, take a romantic Madu River safari through mangrove forests, visit the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery to witness conservation efforts, explore the lush Brief Garden and elegant Lunuganga Estate. Optional water sports add adventure for couples seeking thrills by the ocean.

DAY 05, DAY 06 & DAY 07 | GALLE / UNAWATUNA (3 NIGHTS)

Continue to Unawatuna for extended romantic beach escapades. Relax at Unawatuna Beach and Jungle Beach, hand-in-hand walks through historic Galle Fort, capture perfect moments at Dalawella Beach with its iconic palm swings. Enjoy snorkeling, diving, and seasonal whale watching nearby. Three nights allow for unhurried beach time and adventure.

DAY 08 & DAY 09 | RAINFOREST ESCAPE (2 NIGHTS)

Escape to the misty mountains near Sinharaja Rainforest for nature's romance. Hike through lush greenery and encounter exotic birds and wildlife. Visit cascading waterfalls including Duvili Ella and Bramana Ella. Relax with stunning tea estate views, soak in river baths, enjoy lodge privacy, and savor mountain air. A peaceful conclusion before departure.

DAY 10 | RETURN TO AIRPORT

Transfer to the airport for your departure, carrying with you beautiful memories of your unforgettable 10-day honeymoon across Sri Lanka's most romantic destinations.
