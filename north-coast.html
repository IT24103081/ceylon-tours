NORTH COAST PACKAGE

Year-round 10-day 9-night north coast journey with quiet beaches, rich culture, wildlife, and peaceful coastal escapes.

10-DAY NORTH COAST ITINERARY

DAY 01 | NEGOMBO (1 NIGHT)

Begin your journey in Negombo with a sunset beach walk, a lagoon boat ride, and visits to Muthurajawela Wetlands, the Dutch canal, and the fish market.

DAY 02 & DAY 03 | KALPITIYA (2 NIGHTS)

Travel to Kalpitiya for quiet beaches, dolphin watching, kite surfing, and relaxing lagoon boat rides in one of Sri Lanka’s best dolphin-viewing regions.

DAY 04 & DAY 05 | WILPATTU (2 NIGHTS)

Continue to Wilpattu National Park for an exciting safari among villus and wildlife, with chances to spot leopards, elephants, sloth bears, deer, and birds.

DAY 06 & DAY 07 | MANNAR (2 NIGHTS)

Travel to Mannar to explore Adam’s Bridge, Mannar Fort, and peaceful landscapes, with excellent bird watching during migration seasons.

DAY 08, DAY 09 & DAY 10 | JAFFNA (3 NIGHTS)

Continue to Jaffna for cultural exploration, visiting Nallur Kandaswamy Kovil, Jaffna Fort, nearby islands, quiet beaches, and traditional northern cuisine.

RETURN TO NEGOMBO | DEPARTURE

Return to Negombo for your departure and relax before your flight after an unforgettable north coast journey.
