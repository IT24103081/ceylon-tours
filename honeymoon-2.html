ROMANTIC ESCAPE

HONEYMOON PACKAGE 2

Celebrate your love with a beautiful journey combining golden beaches, wildlife adventures, misty mountains, and cultural experiences.

DURATION 10 Days / 9 Nights
STYLE Romance, Nature & Cultural
STATUS Complete Journey

ROMANCE, NATURE & SCENIC ESCAPE

DAY 01 | NEGOMBO (1 NIGHT)

Begin your honeymoon in Negombo, a charming coastal town close to the airport. Relax at Negombo Beach and enjoy a romantic sunset walk along the shore. Take a peaceful boat ride through Negombo Lagoon, surrounded by calm waters and birdlife. Explore the natural beauty of Muthurajawela Wetlands, a quiet and scenic wetland rich in wildlife. You can also visit the Dutch canal and the lively fish market to experience local culture together.

DAY 02 & DAY 03 | TANGALLE (2 NIGHTS)

Travel to Tangalle, known for its peaceful and less crowded beaches—perfect for couples. Relax on beautiful beaches such as Tangalle Beach and Silent Beach, ideal for quiet moments, sunbathing, and romantic walks. You can also visit nearby lagoons or simply enjoy your time at a luxury beachside resort, surrounded by nature and the sound of the ocean.

DAY 04 | UDAWALAWE (1 NIGHT)

Continue to Udawalawe for a unique wildlife experience. Enjoy an exciting safari at Udawalawe National Park, one of the best places to see elephants in their natural habitat. You may also spot deer, buffalo, crocodiles, and many bird species. After the safari, relax at your peaceful villa near the park, surrounded by nature.

DAY 05 & DAY 06 | ELLA (2 NIGHTS)

Travel to Ella, a small mountain town known for its breathtaking views and romantic atmosphere. Visit the famous Nine Arches Bridge and enjoy scenic walks in the surrounding greenery. Hike to Little Adam's Peak for stunning sunrise or sunset views. Explore Ravana Falls and Ravana Cave, and enjoy adventurous activities at Flying Ravana Adventure Park. Ella offers a perfect mix of relaxation, adventure, and unforgettable scenery for couples.

DAY 07 & DAY 08 | KANDY (2 NIGHTS)

Continue to Kandy, a beautiful city surrounded by hills and culture. Visit the sacred Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic, an important spiritual site. Enjoy a relaxing walk at Royal Botanic Gardens, Peradeniya, known for its lush greenery and peaceful environment. You can also explore Kandy city, enjoy lake views, and experience local culture together.

DAY 09 | NEGOMBO (1 NIGHT)

Return to Negombo for your final night. Relax by the beach, enjoy a romantic dinner, and reflect on your unforgettable honeymoon journey together.

DAY 10 | DEPARTURE

Transfer to the airport for your departure, taking with you beautiful memories of your honeymoon in Sri Lanka.
