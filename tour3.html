Tour 3

COASTAL BEACH LOOP — 9 DAYS / 8 NIGHTS

A coastal route from Galle to Arugam Bay, Pasikudah, Nilaveli and Kalpitiya before ending in Negombo and the airport.

TOUR ITINERARY

DAY 01 | AIRPORT to GALLE

Arrive in Sri Lanka and transfer to Galle for a relaxed coastal start to the journey.

DAY 02 | GALLE to MIRISSA

Continue along the southern coast to Mirissa for beaches, downtime and ocean views.

DAY 03 | MIRISSA to ARUGAMBAY

Travel across the island to Arugam Bay for the east coast’s surf and beach atmosphere.

DAY 04 | ARUGAMBAY to PASIKUDA

Move north along the east coast to Pasikudah for calm shallow waters and a restful stay.

DAY 05 | PASIKUDA to NILAVELI

Continue to Nilaveli for more coastal scenery and easy access to the Trincomalee area.

DAY 06 | NILAVELI to KALPITIYA

Travel across the island to Kalpitiya, known for long beaches, lagoons and a peaceful coastal setting.

DAY 07 | KALPITIYA to NEGOMBO

Return to Negombo for your final night near the airport and some relaxed time before departure.

DAY 08 | NEGOMBO to AIRPORT

Transfer to Bandaranaike International Airport for departure.

ROUTE MAP

GOOD TO KNOW

Bring swimwear, light clothing and sun protection for the coastal route.
