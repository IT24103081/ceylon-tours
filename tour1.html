Tour 1

CLASSIC SRI LANKA CIRCUIT — 12 DAYS / 11 NIGHTS

A private 12-day journey through Sri Lanka’s ancient cities, misty tea country and sun-kissed southern beaches — culture, wildlife and coastal relaxation.

TOUR ITINERARY

DAY 01 | AIRPORT to NEGOMBO

Arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport and transfer to Negombo for an overnight stay and recovery from the flight.

DAY 02 | NEGOMBO to ANURADHAPURA

Drive to the ancient city of Anuradhapura. Explore stupas, the sacred Bodhi tree and archaeological ruins.

DAY 03 | ANURADHAPURA to SIGIRIYA

Transfer to Sigiriya. Optional climb to the Sigiriya Rock Fortress and visit local village sites.

DAY 04 | SIGIRIYA to POLONNARUWA

Drive to Polonnaruwa and tour the medieval capital by bike or tuk-tuk, visiting Gal Vihara and Parakrama Samudra.

DAY 05 | POLONNARUWA to KANDY

Travel to Kandy. In the evening visit the Temple of the Tooth and stroll the Kandy Lake precinct.

DAY 06 | KANDY to NUWARA ELIYA

Journey into tea country. Visit a tea factory and enjoy cool hill-station air in Nuwara Eliya.

DAY 07 | NUWARA ELIYA to ELLA

Scenic travel to Ella; optional hikes to Little Adam's Peak or Nine Arch Bridge.

DAY 08 | ELLA to YALA

Drive south to Yala National Park for an afternoon/evening safari to spot leopards and elephants.

DAY 09 | YALA to MIRISSA

Transfer to Mirissa for beach time; optional whale-watching (seasonal) or relax on the sand.

DAY 10 | MIRISSA to GALLE / UNAWATUNA

Short drive to Galle Fort and Unawatuna; explore the historic ramparts and nearby beaches.

DAY 11 | GALLE to NEGOMBO

Return north to Negombo for an overnight near the airport and a leisurely evening by the lagoon.

DAY 12 | NEGOMBO to AIRPORT

Transfer to Bandaranaike International Airport for departure.

GOOD TO KNOW

Pack light layers for hill country; temperatures vary.

Temples require modest dress; remove shoes before entering.

Wildlife sightings are never guaranteed — be patient and arrive early for safaris.
